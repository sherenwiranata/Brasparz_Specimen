Brasparz is a modular geometric display typeface by Charlie Le Maignan. Inspired by Vincent Trevor’s lettering and the bold elegance of Art Deco, it brings structure and style to contemporary design.

The typeface balances sharp contrasts with softer edges, giving it an architectural rhythm that still feels warm and approachable. Its modular build keeps forms consistent while staying flexible across use cases.

Brasparz comes with initial caps, decorative alternates, and plenty of room for play. It doesn’t shy away from ornament — it invites it.
