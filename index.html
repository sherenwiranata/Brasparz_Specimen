Brasparz

Brasparz is a geometric font inspired by Vincent Trevor’s font and Art Deco. It has high contrasts, slightly rounded edges, and comes in 5 weights across one variable axis. It also includes initial caps and many alternates. No matter what, Brasparz will never fear ornaments.

  • Designer: Charlie Le Maignan
  • Foundry: Charlie Le Maignan
  • Release: 2025
